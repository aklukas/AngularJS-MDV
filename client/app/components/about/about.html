Master Detail View for AngularJS

  This is an example of what we here at Venturplex call,
a master detail view.

Though a simple app, you have full CRUD. This is the starting block of most web applications today.

  1. You have a payload coming in from a public API with a service, then being dynamically listed out aka READ.
  2. You also have a form or "detail" for CREATE or UPDATE.
  3. Then on the list you can DELETE.
In just a few steps you have a full CRUD app using AngularJS and HTTP.
